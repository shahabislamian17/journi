Itinerary

Pg. de la Mar Meeting Point

Gather at Pg. de la Mar, meet your crew, receive a warm welcome, safety briefing, and prepare for the sailing adventure.

Espalmador 1 Hour

Sail toward pristine Espalmador, an unspoiled island paradise with white sands, crystal waters, and time to swim, relax, and explore freely.

Illetes & Lunch 2 Hours

Anchor at stunning Illetes, one of the world's best beaches, where a delicious onboard lunch awaits alongside relaxation and breathtaking views.

Pg. de la Mar Departure Point

Return sail to Pg. de la Mar, capturing last memories of Formentera's beauty before a smooth, heartfelt farewell departure.

What's Included
